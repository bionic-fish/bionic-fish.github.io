Case Study : Travel Brochure

World adventurer, traveling tourist, family vacation? At NorthWest we offer a wide range of travel packages, accommodation deals, sightseeing adventures and much more!

The Travel Brochure produces a clear and informative approach to searching for the latest travel options, deals, locations, ancient sites & popular tourist attractions.

Displaying attractive user interactive gallery components for ease of use, search functions for party capitals & popular destinations, private getaways and spa packages.


Travel Brochure Website

Overview


A website developed based on my love for travelling, exploring culture and ancient temples, discovering quaint ski towns, and lazy relaxation beach getaways. The website features an interactive search option incorporate based application utilising JavaScript's reactive and interactive features. The application takes a users input from the two search parameters provided in the form of an input element. After completing the form and clicking "save", a JavaScript element will be created under the "Saved Bookmarks" heading. This element houses data taken from the browsers local Storage and displays it to a user with a visit url button and a delete button.
